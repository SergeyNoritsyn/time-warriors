<p><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">Charleville Musket</span></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11px;">The Charleville musket was a .69 caliber standard French infantry musket used in the 18th and 19th centuries and extensively throughout the Napoleonic wars.</p>
<p>Known for its reliability and easy manufacture, this weapon is very useful for standardizing the weaponry of a large army and providing a trusty weapon.</span></p>
