<p><strong><span style="font-size: 19px;">Twelve-Pounder Cannon</span></strong></p>
<p>The 12-Pounder cannon was the heaviest cannon used by the French during the Napoleonic wars.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11px;">Known for its range and great shrapnel damage, its shells caused immense damage to advancing enemy formations.</span></p>
