<p><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">Baker Rifle</span></strong></p>
<p>The Baker rifle was a flintlock rifle used by the rifle regiments of the British Army during the Napoleonic Wars. </p> 
<p><span style="font-size: 11px;">Known for its long range and precision, its rifled bore allowed it to be used to deadly effect by the skirmishers of the English avant-garde.</span></p>
